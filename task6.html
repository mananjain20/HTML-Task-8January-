What are grid lines?
Grid lines are the invisible horizontal and vertical lines that divide a grid into rows and columns. They are used to position and align grid items inside the grid layout.
How many columns does the item span?
A grid item spans as many columns as you specify using grid-column: start / end. By default, it spans 1 column if nothing is set.
What happens to other items?
Other items shift automatically to the next available grid cells. They do not overlap unless you explicitly position them to do so.
Can two items overlap?
By default, grid items cannot overlap, unless specifically forced to.
Where is column spanning used in websites?
Column spanning is used on websites to make elements stretch across multiple columns—for example, a header, banner, or featured section that spans the full width while other content stays in smaller columns.
