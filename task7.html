Why are template areas easy to understand?
Template areas are easy to understand because they visually name sections of the grid, making the layout clear.
Which section spans full width?
The header and footer span the full width.
Can we rearrange layout by changing area names?
Yes, changing area names automatically rearranges grid items without touching the HTML.

Is this better than column numbers?
Yes, template areas are clearer and easier to maintain than remembering grid lines.
Where are template areas mostly used?
Template areas are mostly used in page layouts for headers, sidebars, main content, and footers.
