What does fr stand for?
fr stands for fraction.
How many columns are created?
By default, only 1 column is created when you use Grid without defining any columns.
What happens when the screen size is reduced?
The columns become narrower as the space decreases
Do items overflow or adjust?
They adjust, they do not overflow.
Why is fr better than px for layouts?
fr is better than px because it distributes available space proportionally and adapts to screen size, making layouts flexible and responsive, while px is fixed and does not adjust to different screen widths.
